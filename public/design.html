Type

Sora for headings and display, Inter for body and UI, JetBrains Mono for all data and identifiers, Zilla Slab reserved solely for the wordmark.

Body large — Inter 400 · 17px

Body — Inter 400 · 15px
Standardizing histology stains through reproducible cell-line plugs. Investigational decision-support — not a substitute for pathologist interpretation.
