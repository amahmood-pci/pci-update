System Engineering

Under the Hood: OpenSlide & AI Networks

How our cloud-hosted engine parses multi-gigabyte scans on the fly.

01

Whole Slide Tiling

Pathologist uploads a massive scanner file (e.g., SVS or NDPI). Our backend uses OpenSlide to split the gigapixel array into local 256x256 tiles, loading only what is navigated.

02

Stain Deconvolution

The mathematical algorithm separates stained colors by isolating violet cell nuclei (Hematoxylin) from biomarker protein expression (brown DAB) into separate pixel matrices.

03

Segmental Validation

Neural networks draw cell boundaries, evaluate stain densities against calibrated reference cell plugs, and calculate tumor proportion scores.
